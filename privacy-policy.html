Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2024

Welcome to Tesla Wrap AI. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a personalized design experience and secure account management, we collect the following data:

  • Account Information: Your email address provided during sign-up.
  • Identifiers: A unique User ID assigned to your account to link your designs and preferences.
  • Purchase Data: Information about your subscription status and purchase history (e.g., Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly Premium plans).
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, such as designs created, models selected (e.g., Cybertruck, Model Y), and AI prompt usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To authenticate your account, secure your profile, and enable core features.
  • Product Personalization: To store your custom wrap designs in your personal library and synchronize them across your devices.
  • Subscription Management: To verify your premium status and grant access to AI features.
  • Analytics: To understand feature popularity and improve the overall user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard third-party providers to ensure high-quality service and data security:

  • Supabase: Used for secure user authentication and database management. Your credentials and designs are stored using their encrypted infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions and verify purchase receipts with the Apple App Store.
  • Expo: The framework used to build and maintain the application's mobile performance.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information as long as your account is active. You have the full right to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Profile > Delete Account menu within the app. Upon deletion, all your saved designs and personal info will be permanently removed from our Supabase database.

5. Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We use Supabase's secure authentication protocols to safeguard your account.
